# Guelta Zerka in Guelma: A Forgotten Paradise in Hammam N'Bail

## Introduction:
Are you looking for a sanctuary that combines the silence of the mountains and the roar of clear waters away from the noise of the cities? In the heart of Guelma province, specifically among the folds of the Hammam N'Bail mountains, hides the "Blue Gem" that has not yet been touched by the hand of change. Guelta Zerka waterfalls are not just a tourist site, but an artistic painting painted by nature over thousands of years, where the blueness of the water meets the solidity of the rocks and the greenery of the forests, to offer a unique and unforgettable experience for adventure and relaxation enthusiasts.

![View of Hammam N'Bail Mountains](assets/img/blog/guelta-zerka-5.jpg)

Guelta Zerka waterfalls in Hammam N'Bail are a hidden gem in the heart of the mountains of Guelma province. It is named so because of its water color which tends to clear blue under the sun, and it is an ideal destination for lovers of pure nature and adventure.

________________________________________
๐ Guelta Zerka Waterfalls: A Paradise on Earth
These waterfalls are located in a rugged and charming mountain range, where water falls from the mountain heights reaching up to 20 meters to pour into natural rocky basins (Gueltat) characterized by very cold and refreshing water, especially in summer.

![Guelta Zerka Waterfall](assets/img/blog/guelta-zerka-2.jpg)

What distinguishes the place?
โข **Three consecutive waterfalls:** The site consists of three consecutive water falls pouring into a small lake known as "Guelta Zerka".
โข **Virgin nature:** The place is surrounded by dense forests and high mountains such as "Ras El Alia" mountain (1317m) and "Djebel Chramet".
โข **A haven for healing:** Many visit it seeking fresh air and psychological comfort amidst the silence of nature.

๐ Access Guide (How to get to Guelta Zerka?)
Guelta Zerka is about 40 km northeast of Guelma city.

![Hiking towards the Guelta](assets/img/blog/guelta-zerka-1.jpg)

๐ By Private Car:
1. Start from Guelma city via National Road No. 20 (RN20) towards the east of the province.
2. Upon reaching the crossroads leading to Hammam N'Bail, take Provincial Road No. 19 (CW19).
3. After reaching the center of Hammam N'Bail municipality, you will need to head towards the "El Mchaala" area.
4. Important warning: The road in the last meters may be rugged and not fully paved, so it is preferable to use high or 4WD cars.

๐ถ Hiking Path:
After parking the car at the nearest possible point, you will have to walk through mountain trails for 30 to 60 minutes to reach the main waterfall. The trail requires physical effort and is ideal for "sports hiking" enthusiasts.

โบ Tourist Activities You Can Do
1. **Swimming and Relaxation:** The waterfall water is very cold and suitable for swimming on hot days (despite sometimes official warnings to keep the place clean).
2. **Photography:** The site provides stunning panoramic angles, especially at "Djebel Chramet" which overlooks the entire area.
3. **Camping:** The area is a destination for mountain camping enthusiasts, but care must be taken to bring all supplies as there are no nearby commercial facilities.
4. **Exploring "Damous El Hssan":** A cave near the Gueltat that tourists prefer to explore during their trip.
